About Us

Welcome to the Kenya Primary School Examination Management System.This platform is designed to automate and streamline the examination results storage,analysis,and generation of reports ; providing a centralized system for managing student registrations, exam results, and analysis.

Our goal is to ensure accuracy, efficiency, and accessibility in managing primary school exams across Kenya.

We provide pupil progress tracking and analysis,allowing parents, guardians and sponsors to know and understand problem areas. Allowing each kenyan school going child a chance for sucess using this refined process.
